Package Description

If you would like to bring your yoga practice to the next level, you may opt for one-on-one personalized teaching with your preferred yoga instructor.

This may be unfamiliar to you if you’ve only participated in group-level classes, but practitioners swear it makes a huge difference! We particularly recommend one-on-one classes for:
